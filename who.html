Who am I??
 

My name is Smadar Naoz. I am a PhD student in the Department of Astronomy and Astrophysics , which is part of the School of Physics and Astronomy at the Tel Aviv University . My PhD advisor is Rennan Barkana.

Previously I was a master student at the Racah institute at The Hebrew University. My advisor was Nir Shaviv.

The main subject in my PhD studies is fluctuations in 21cm. More about my research can be found here .

The meaning of the name Naoz:

Naos (yes, with "s") is a star about 430pc from us, in the constellation Puppies. Naos in Greek means ship. Moshe, my husband, and I chose this name because we both are amateur astronomers, and the word ship is suitable for our journey together.
