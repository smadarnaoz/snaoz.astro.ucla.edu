The meaning of the name Naoz:

Naos (with an s) is a star about 500pc from us, in the constellation Puppies. Naos in Greek means ship. Moshe, my husband, and I chose this name because we both are amateur astronomers, and the word ship is suitable for our journey together. We have changed it to Naoz (this time with a z) so it will sound like a word in Hebrew.
