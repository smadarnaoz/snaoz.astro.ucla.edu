6)  Asymptotic Giant Branch (AGB):   Core is fully converted to carbon and oxygen.
No fusion takes place in the core. A helium burning shell is surrounded by a hydrogen
burning shell. The core contracts, heats up, and becomes degenerate.
Energy production rate increases  due to increased temperature/density in the shells.
An enormous expansion again occurs and the star becomes a red super giant.
