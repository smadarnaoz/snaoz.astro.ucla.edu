“...My major theme has been that all of the heavy elements from carbon
to uranium have been synthesized in stars.  Let me remind you that your
bodies consist for the most part of these heavy elements.  Apart from
hydrogen you are 65 percent oxygen and 18 percent carbon with smaller
percentages of nitrogen, magnesium, phosphorous, sulphur, chlorine,
potassium, and traces of still heavier elements.
“Thus it is possible to say that you and your neighbor and I, each one of
us and all of us, are truly and literally a little bit of stardust!...”
                                                        W. Fowler 1983
                                                        Nobel Lecture
