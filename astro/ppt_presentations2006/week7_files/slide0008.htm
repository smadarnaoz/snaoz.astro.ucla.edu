2)  Sub-Giant Phase.  Core is fully converted to 4He. No fusion takes place
                                    in the helium core, so it contracts and heats up.
     Hydrogen burning (4p à 4He) occurs in a surrounding shell.
     Energy production rate increases due to increasing temperature/density in the shell.
     However, extra energy cant get out (escape limited by radiative diffusion rate),
     so the star expands at fixed luminosity, and the surface temperature drops.
