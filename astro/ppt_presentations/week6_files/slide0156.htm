White dwarf properties are beautiful (and simple) consequences
of quantum mechanics, thermodynamics, and special relativity.
Goals:
   1) understand “degenerate electron pressure” for
       non-relativistic and ultra-relativistic gases.
   2) derive an exact formula for the mass-radius relation
       for a non-relativistic degenerate gas.
   3) show that a mass-radius relation is impossible for
       a relativistic gas, leading to the Chandrasekhar limit.
