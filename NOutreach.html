Outreach


Reaching out to the community provides opportunities for the general community and young people to engage, learn, and explore. I have a long history of educational outreach, ranging from giving public talks at schools and museums to designing specific programs for women, minorities and under-represented groups in science.

  • An article I wrote for The Conversation
  • Da Mada (דע מדע) podcast (Hebrew)
  • A farwell to Stephen Hawking (Hebrew) Davar Rishon article I wrote
  • How to Flip a Planet video, as part of David Kipping's Cool Worlds outreach YouTube channel.
  • Astronomy for Everyone - A neuro-diversity workshop for high school students
  • CfA Career workshops for young people who think differently for young people with dyslexia, ADHD and autism spectrum disorders
  • Astroclub
  • Astropedia
